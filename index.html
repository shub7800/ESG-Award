Dear Sir or Madam,

We are delighted to inform you that the IBAAS is organizing the 13th International Bauxite, Alumina & Aluminium Conference & Exhibition (IBAAS-IMMT-IIM 2025). This prestigious event will take place from October 8–10, 2025, at CSIR-IMMT, Bhubaneswar, India.

This leading platform for the aluminium industry in India will host over 70 companies and about 300 delegates globally. We proudly introduce the IBAAS 2025 ESG (Environmental, Social & Governance) Awards, jointly organized with CETIZION Verifica, to honour excellence in sustainability and governance within the aluminium sector.

The second day will feature a special session by Aluminium Stewardship Initiative (ASI) on sustainability and decarbonization in the aluminium industry.

We invite your esteemed organization to participate in the ESG Awards and share your achievements and ESG commitment with a global audience.
